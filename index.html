The process

Up and running
in 3 steps

01

Install Smart Sensors

Our technicians install non-invasive IoT sensors on your existing looms, dyeing machines and knitting equipment. Zero downtime during installation — your plant keeps running.

02

Connect to the Platform

Data flows in real-time to your DyeTex dashboard. See every machine's status, efficiency score and anomaly indicators from any device, anywhere.

03

Act on Intelligence

Receive predictive alerts, schedule maintenance, generate reports and make decisions backed by data — before a single stitch is lost.
